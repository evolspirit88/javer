Our Framework

The CLEAR
Methodology.

Taking on the new era of digital marketing — five principles governing everything we do.

The Problem

Digital marketing is confusing.

Privacy Rules

With growing privacy concerns, browsers and devices are shifting their policies. Third-party cookies are being phased out, and apps must obtain agreement to track and re-target users across platforms.

Blurred Lines

There used to be a difference between creative branding and data science. As creative marketers migrate online and digital marketers invest in brand awareness, the lines have blurred — and both must work together.

Rising Standards

The COVID-19 pandemic altered how individuals consumed media. Creative quality and a flawless user experience are now the standard — not a differentiator.

More Competition

With more corporations moving online and the rise of direct-to-consumer brands, competition for digital shelf space has intensified. More competition means higher acquisition costs.

Platform Complexity

Buying journeys have never been more complex due to the enormous variety of platforms, publishers, channels, and touchpoints. Connecting the dots is harder than ever.

The Solution

Introducing CLEAR.

Through our brand and performance hybrid approach, we leave a lasting impression without losing sight of ROI. The best marketing does both.
